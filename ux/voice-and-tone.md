## Brand Voice

Our voice is the consistent personality behind all Sacred Vows communications. It remains constant across all touchpoints.

### Voice Characteristics

| Attribute | Description | Example |
|-----------|-------------|---------|
| **Warm** | Friendly and caring, like a supportive friend | "We're so excited to be part of your journey" |
| **Elegant** | Sophisticated without being stuffy | "Create timeless elegance" not "Make fancy invites" |
| **Helpful** | Solution-oriented and supportive | "Here's how to fix that" not "Error occurred" |
| **Celebratory** | Joyful but not over-the-top | "Beautiful! Your guests will love it" |
| **Confident** | Assured without being arrogant | "You're in good hands" |

### We Are...
- Warm and encouraging
- Helpful and knowledgeable
- Celebratory but tasteful
- Professional yet personal
- Romantic but not cheesy

### We Are Not...
- Pushy or salesy
- Cold or corporate
- Overly casual or silly
- Condescending
- Generic or robotic

## Tone Spectrum

While our voice stays consistent, our tone adapts to the situation.

### Informational (Neutral)
For documentation, FAQs, and educational content.
> "Your invitation link can be shared via text, email, or social media. Each guest who opens the link will see your personalized design."

### Encouraging (Warm)
For onboarding, tutorials, and positive feedback.
> "You're doing great! Just a few more details and your invitation will be ready to share with the world."

### Celebratory (Enthusiastic)
For success states and achievements.
> "Congratulations! Your beautiful invitation is now live. Your guests are going to love it! ðŸŽ‰"

### Supportive (Empathetic)
For errors, issues, and support interactions.
> "We understand how important this is. Don't worryâ€”we're here to help you get back on track."

### Urgent (Direct)
For critical errors or time-sensitive information.
> "Action needed: Please verify your email to continue editing your invitation."

## Channel-Specific Guidelines

### Website Copy
- Benefit-focused headlines
- Scannable paragraphs (3-4 sentences max)
- Clear CTAs

### Email
- Personal, warm greeting
- Single clear purpose per email
- Easy unsubscribe

### Error Messages
- Never blame the user
- Provide solutions
- Include a way to get help

### Support Chat
- Conversational but professional
- Quick acknowledgment of issue
- Follow up on resolution

## Terminology

### Use These Terms
| Term | Instead of |
|------|------------|
| Wedding invitation | E-vite, digital card |
| Layout | Theme, skin |
| Guests | Invitees, recipients |
| RSVP | Response, reply |
| Dashboard | Control panel, admin |
| Share | Send, distribute |

### Words to Embrace
- Beautiful
- Elegant
- Personalized
- Celebrate
- Love story
- Special day
- Joy

### Words to Avoid
- Cheap (use "affordable")
- Easy (use "simple" or "effortless")
- Hassle-free
- Best-in-class
- Cutting-edge
- Leverage
- Utilize (use "use")
- Synergy
